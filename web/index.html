Judy McLeish, President

Judy founded McDaniel Partners to provide clients with experienced advice that could be provided in partnership with clients. Her philosophy of consulting stresses the importance of accountability, partnering, sharing advice and developing existing employees.

Profile

McDaniel Partners is a management consulting services company headquartered in Toronto, Canada.
