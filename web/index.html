Our Team

We bring more than five decades of experience to all clients



Judy McLeish, President

Judy is the founder and President of McDaniel Partners. Judy has over 25 years of consulting experience working with a wide range of companies in Canada, the United States, London, The Netherlands, and The Caribbean.

Judy founded McDaniel Partners to provide clients with experienced advice that could be provided in partnership with clients. Her philosophy of consulting stresses the importance of accountability, partnering, sharing advice and developing existing employees.

Jennifer Kelly, Branding Expert

Jennifer has been with McDaniel Partners since 2004. Jennifer has over 25 years of experience working with organizations to develop and deliver unique brand experiences to both their customers and employees.

Jennifer brings our clients extensive private and public sector experience and has successfully implemented complex strategies and initiatives in Canada, the U.S. and the Caribbean.

Mark Clarkson, Graphics & Design

Mark has 23 years of experience in the creative side of computing, providing everything from web development to 3D animation for clients throughout North America and Europe.

Mark has created award-winning 2D animations and 3D graphics for clients ranging from Megadeth to Microsoft.

Mark is the author of 7 books, including "Photoshop Secrets of the Pros."

Our Advisors



Christine Croucher

Christine Croucher has had an outstanding 26-year career, during which time she has taken charge of initiatives such as high-growth strategies, financial planning and management, as well as sales management.

Christine brings strong financial and call centre experience, gained through several years with CIBC and previous to that at Canadian Tire Acceptance to McDaniel Partners. Christine has also served as a Board member of Visa Canada, Visa International, Juniper (a US credit card company) and the Interac Association.

Gail Kilgour

Gail Kilgour is a graduate of York University and the University of Waterloo. She brings more than 25 years of experience in the financial services industry. Gail also currently serves on the Board of Trustees for Royal LePage Franchise Services Trust and the Ontario Realty Corporation.

She is formerly President and CEO of EDULINX Canada Corporation; Senior Vice-President, Government Sponsored Student Loans, Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce and Senior Vice-President, e-Business Strategy, CIBC.Com, CIBC.

She has been an active fundraiser serving on the Capital Campaign Committee for York University and Wellesley Hospital and the Development Committee at Kingsway College School.

Grant McDaniel

Grant has over 20 years of experience in selling complex deals to the financial services industry. Grant is currently with EDS Canada and has experience at AT&T Canada and Symcor.



David J. McLeish

David has over 37 years in the investment business. In 1997, David joined Octagon Capital as Chairman and CEO and grew the company from 8 persons to approximately 100 at present. David resigned as Chairman in August 2007, but currently remains at the company and serves on the Board of Directors of FTG Corporation.

Our Profile

McDaniel Partners is a management consulting services company headquartered in Toronto, Canada.

We focus on providing practical solutions that create meaningful, measurable results.

We were founded in 2002 on the principle that companies need partners as well as experts; that companies benefit from a consulting partner who intimately understands their industry, their internal challenges and can provide efficient solutions and options that deliver sustainable, long-term results.
